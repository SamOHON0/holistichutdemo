About Margaret

My own journey led me here.

I'm Margaret Brown. For years I suffered with severe migraines and saw specialist after specialist, but it was complementary therapy that finally helped. That's where my love of reflexology began, and why I trained, first as a reflexologist, then in Reiki, holistic massage, Indian head massage and aromatherapy.

I've now run The Holistic Hut from my home in Castleknock for four wonderful years. Every treatment is tailored to you, and I take real care to make the space warm and welcoming from the moment you arrive.

Fully qualified: ITEC diplomas, Reiki Master, and member of the National Register of Reflexologists Ireland

Specialist training in reflexology for maternity, fertility, menopause and complementary cancer care

A private, comfortable treatment room at home, with parking right outside
